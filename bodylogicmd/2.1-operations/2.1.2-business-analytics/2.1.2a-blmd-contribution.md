# 2.1.2a BLMD Contribution

## Auto-Order Supplements

* If the order is place in oneRx
  * Wholesale - Cost + \(9.95 -11\)
* Otherwise
  * Wholesale - Discount - Cost + \(9.95 - 11\)

## DFO Supplements

* Wholesale - Discount - Cost - 20

## Programs

* Wholesale - Cost - Discount + Shipping
